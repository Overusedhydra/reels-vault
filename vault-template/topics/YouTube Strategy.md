# YouTube Strategy

Growing on YouTube requires understanding the algorithm, retention, and audience psychology.

## Core Principles

- **Retention is king** — Watch time drives the algorithm
- **CTR × Retention = Growth** — Need both
- **Consistency** — Upload schedule matters
- **Niche down** — Be known for one thing first
- **Session time** — Get people to watch more videos

## The Algorithm

YouTube recommends videos based on:
1. **Click-through rate (CTR)** — Thumbnail + title
2. **Average view duration (AVD)** — How long they watch
3. **Average percentage viewed** — Completion rate
4. **Session time** — Do they watch more after?
5. **Engagement** — Likes, comments, shares

## Title Formulas

- **Number + Adjective + Keyword + Promise:** "7 Insane Ways to Double Your Income"
- **How to + Desired outcome:** "How to Get 10K Followers in 30 Days"
- **Why + Contrarian:** "Why Morning Routines Are Overrated"
- **Mistake + Target:** "5 Mistakes Killing Your Content"

## Retention Tactics

### First 30 Seconds
- Restate the title promise
- Preview what they'll learn
- Create an open loop

### Middle
- Pattern interrupt every 30-60 seconds
- B-roll to illustrate points
- Callbacks to earlier hooks

### End
- Summarize key takeaways
- Strong CTA (subscribe, watch next)
- End screen with related videos

## Upload Strategy

| Frequency | Best For |
|-----------|----------|
| Daily | Shorts, building habit |
| 2-3x/week | Long-form, quality focus |
| 1x/week | Minimum for growth |
| 1x/month | Only if production value is high |
