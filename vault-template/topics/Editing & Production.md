# Editing & Production

How to edit content so it holds attention and looks professional.

## Core Principles

- **Cut the dead air** — Remove every pause, "um", "uh"
- **Pattern every 3-5 seconds** — Change angle, add B-roll, text overlay
- **Audio is 50%** — Bad audio = instant scroll away
- **Match energy to content** — Fast cuts for energy, slow for emphasis
- **Subtitles always** — 80%+ of people watch on mute

## Short-Form Editing Rules

### Reels/TikTok/Shorts
- **Length:** 15-90 seconds sweet spot
- **Cuts:** Every 2-4 seconds
- **Text:** On-screen text reinforcing key points
- **Music:** Background music at 20-30% volume
- **Zoom:** Punch in on emphasis points
- **Speed:** 1.2x-1.5x for talking head

### YouTube
- **Length:** 8-15 minutes for algo, 3-5 min for Shorts
- **Cuts:** Every 5-8 seconds
- **B-roll:** Show what you're talking about
- **Thumbnails:** A/B test with title
- **Chapters:** Help retention + SEO
