# Short-Form Video

Reels, TikToks, Shorts — the dominant format for reach and discovery.

## Core Principles

- **Hook in 0.5 seconds** — Not 3, half a second
- **Loop potential** — End where it can start again
- **Trend surfing** — Use trending sounds/formats
- **Native feel** — Don't look overproduced
- **One idea per video** — Don't overload

## By Creator

### [[Alex Hormozi]] — Volume Play
- Film many hooks, edit together
- Test 150-750 variations per week
- Speed > perfection

### [[Gary Vaynerchuk]] — Native Content
- Recut for each platform
- Trend surfing at scale
- "60 pieces of content from one keynote"

### [[Ali Abdaal]] — Structured Short-Form
- Hook → Value → CTA
- Consistent posting schedule
- Use Shorts to drive long-form

## Content Types That Work

1. **Tutorials** — "How to do X in 30 seconds"
2. **Before/After** — Transformation content
3. **POV** — Point of view storytelling
4. **Storytime** — Personal narrative
5. **Reaction** — Duet/stitch trends
6. **Listicle** — "3 things you didn't know about..."
7. **Day in the life** — Behind the scenes

## Viral Formula

```
Trending sound + Your niche twist + Strong hook + Loop ending
```

## Posting Strategy

- **Frequency:** 1-3x daily for growth
- **Best times:** 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-9 PM (audience timezone)
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant, not spammy
- **Captions:** Short, engagement-driving question

## Engagement Tactics

- Ask questions in captions
- Reply to comments with videos
- Create "part 2" chains
- Use polls in stories
- Collaborate with other creators
