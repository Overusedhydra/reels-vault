# Thumbnails & Visuals

Click-through rate (CTR) determines if your content gets seen. Thumbnails are the #1 lever for YouTube.

## Core Principles

- **3 elements max** — Face + text + object
- **Contrast** — Bright colors, dark outlines
- **Emotion** — Faces with strong expressions
- **Curiosity** — Show enough to intrigue, not enough to satisfy
- **Test everything** — A/B test thumbnails constantly

## By Creator

### [[Ali Abdaal]] — Curiosity Thumbnails
- Expressive face + bold text
- Before/after splits
- "I did X" with result visible

### [[Gary Vaynerchuk]] — Raw & Real
- Less polished, more authentic
- Real moments over designed graphics
- "Document, don't create" applies to thumbnails too

## Thumbnail Formula

```
[Expressive face] + [3-5 word text] + [Relevant object/icon]
```

## YouTube Thumbnail Rules

- **Resolution:** 1280x720 minimum
- **File size:** Under 2MB
- **Text:** Large, readable on mobile
- **Colors:** High saturation, complementary colors
- **Face:** Takes up 30-50% of thumbnail
- **Expression:** Surprise, excitement, concern

## CTR Benchmarks

| CTR | Rating |
|-----|--------|
| 2-4% | Average |
| 5-8% | Good |
| 8-12% | Great |
| 12%+ | Viral potential |

## Visual Content Rules

### Instagram Grid
- Consistent color palette
- Alternate formats (photo, graphic, quote)
- Plan 9 posts ahead for grid aesthetics

### Stories
- Use polls, questions, quizzes
- 15 seconds max per story
- First story = hook
