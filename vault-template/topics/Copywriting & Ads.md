# Copywriting & Ads

Words that sell. From ad copy to landing pages to email sequences.

## Core Principles

- **Benefits over features** — "Save 2 hours" beats "AI-powered"
- **Specificity wins** — "47% more conversions" beats "better results"
- **One CTA per piece** — Don't split attention
- **Social proof** — Testimonials, numbers, logos
- **Urgency** — Real deadlines, not fake scarcity

## By Creator

### [[Alex Hormozi]] — Volume + Testing
- Write 50 hooks, 3-5 meats, 1-3 CTAs
- Test everything, keep what works
- "Variety is the key to volume"

## Ad Frameworks

### Hormozi's Ad Formula
1. **Hook** — Stop the scroll
2. **Meat** — Educate on the offer/problem/solution
3. **CTA** — Tell them exactly what to do

### AIDA for Ads
- **A**ttention — Bold claim or question
- **I**nterest — Why they should care
- **D**esire — What life looks like after
- **A**ction — Clear next step

### Facebook/Meta Ad Structure
- **Primary text** — 125 chars max for feed
- **Headline** — 40 chars
- **Description** — 30 chars
- **CTA button** — Match intent

## Landing Page Copy

### Above the Fold
1. Headline — Clear value proposition
2. Subheadline — Expand on the headline
3. CTA — Primary action
4. Social proof — Logo bar or testimonial

### Below the Fold
1. Problem — Agitate the pain
2. Solution — Your product
3. Benefits — 3-5 key benefits
4. Testimonials — Real results
5. FAQ — Overcome objections
6. Final CTA — Repeat the ask

## Email Sequences

### Welcome Sequence (5 emails)
1. **Welcome** — Deliver the lead magnet, set expectations
2. **Story** — Your origin, why you built this
3. **Value** — Best actionable tip
4. **Social proof** — Case study or testimonial
5. **Soft pitch** — Introduce product

### Cold Email
- **Subject:** 3-5 words, curiosity-driven
- **Body:** 3 sentences max
- **CTA:** One clear ask (call, reply, link)
