# Distribution & Growth

Getting your content in front of the right people, at the right time, on the right platform.

## Core Principles

- **Platform-native content** — Don't cross-post as-is
- **Consistency > virality** — Show up every day
- **Engagement is distribution** — Reply to every comment
- **Collaboration** — Other creators' audiences are your audience
- **Own your audience** — Email list is the only platform you control

## By Creator

### [[Alex Hormozi]] — Volume as Distribution
- More ads = more chances to win
- Variety prevents ad fatigue
- Test across platforms simultaneously

### [[Gary Vaynerchuk]] — Platform-Native
- Every platform has its own culture
- Recut content for each platform
- "Marketers ruin everything" — adapt fast

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Best For | Content Type | Frequency |
|----------|----------|--------------|-----------|
| Instagram | Brand, visuals | Reels, carousels, stories | 1-3x/day |
| TikTok | Discovery, reach | Short-form video | 1-3x/day |
| YouTube | Depth, trust | Long-form + Shorts | 2-3x/week |
| LinkedIn | B2B, professional | Text, articles | 1x/day |
| Twitter/X | Thoughts, threads | Text, threads | 3-5x/day |
| Email | Depth, conversion | Newsletters | 1-2x/week |

## Cross-Platform Repurposing

### YouTube → Everything
1. Upload full video to YouTube
2. Cut 3-5 Shorts from key moments
3. Post Shorts to TikTok + Reels
4. Transcribe → blog post
5. Key points → Twitter thread
6. Professional angle → LinkedIn post
7. Deep dive → Email newsletter

## Growth Levers

### Organic Growth
- SEO (YouTube, blog)
- Hashtags (Instagram, TikTok)
- Collaborations
- Comment engagement
- Trend surfing

### Paid Growth
- Boost top performers
- Retarget engaged users
- Lookalike audiences
- Collaborate with influencers

## Analytics to Track

| Metric | What It Tells You |

| Share rate | How shareable it is |
