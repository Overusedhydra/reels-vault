# Alex Hormozi

Founder of Acquisition.com. Known for high-volume ad systems, direct response copywriting, and no-BS business advice.

## Key Frameworks

### The Ad Volume System
**50 hooks × 3-5 meats × 1-3 CTAs = 150-750 ads per week**

Source: [[Alex Hormozi — Ad Volume Framework]]

### The 5 Hook Sources
1. Winning hooks from previous ads
2. Hooks from your own free content
3. Winning hooks from other people's ads
4. Winning hooks from other people's free content
5. Platform-specific ad libraries

### The 5 Meat Formats
1. Demonstration ads
2. Testimonial ads
3. Educational ads
4. Story ads
5. Faceless ads

### The CTA Formula
- Show and tell them exactly what to do next
- A good CTA tells the viewer 5 things
- Write 1-3 total
