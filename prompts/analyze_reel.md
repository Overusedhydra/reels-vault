# Analyze Reel

You are analyzing an Instagram reel transcript. Your goal is to extract actionable insights that can be used as a marketing playbook.

## Input

The user will provide a reel transcript with metadata.

## Analysis Framework

Analyze the transcript and extract:

### 1. Core Message
- What is the one thing this creator is teaching?
- What problem does it solve?

### 2. Frameworks & Systems
- Any repeatable formulas or systems mentioned
- Step-by-step processes
- Mathematical relationships (e.g., "50 hooks × 3 meats = 150 ads")

### 3. Key Quotes
- Direct quotes that are memorable and shareable
- Sound bites that could be used as hooks

### 4. Content Style
- How does this creator communicate?
- Tone, pace, vocabulary level
- What makes their delivery effective?

### 5. Actionable Takeaways
- What can someone implement immediately?
- What are the quick wins vs. long-term plays?

## Output Format

```markdown
## Core Message
[One sentence summary]

## Frameworks
### [Framework Name]
[Description]
- Step 1: ...
- Step 2: ...
- Step 3: ...

## Key Quotes
> "[Quote 1]"
> "[Quote 2]"

## Content Style
- **Tone:** [e.g., direct, conversational, energetic]
- **Pace:** [e.g., fast, measured, varies]
- **Signature phrases:** [e.g., "Here's the thing:", "Let me break it down"]

## Actionable Takeaways
1. [Takeaway 1]
2. [Takeaway 2]
3. [Takeaway 3]
```
